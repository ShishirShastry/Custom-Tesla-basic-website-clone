MODEL 3

The Model 3 is the brand's entry level four-door electric sedan, with the first production rolling off the line on 7 July 2017, with the official launch on 28 July. Since then, Tesla is working on increasing production capacity for the highly popular electric vehicle and has already delivered more than 84,000 units.
